Mission/Vision

The University of the Cordilleras envisions itself as an innovative resource center that facilitates
the acquisition of knowledge and lifelong learning in the community.
The UC mission is to support the teaching, learning, research and community extension programs of the university through provision of comprehensive information resources and customer-focused services.

Goals and Objectives

Through the years, the University of the Cordilleras has grown bigger through the offering of additional courses and increasing student enrollment. It continues to
grow even better with time, as evidenced by exemplary performance in various fields of discipline, with its graduates shining in national licensure examinations, and its alumni doing well in private employment, in the practice of their trade and profession, and in public service.
